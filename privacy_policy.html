Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

ObjectVault ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed as a local-only storage solution. All data, including:

  • Photos and images
  • Descriptions and location data
  • Security PINs and recovery information

is stored exclusively on your device using a local database (Isar). We do not collect, store, or transmit this data to any external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Used to display non-personalized ads. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers and location data for advertisement frequency capping and reporting.
  • Isar Database: A local database engine that stores your data on your device.

3. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on:

  1. The physical security of your device.
  2. The strength of your device's lock screen.
  3. The App's built-in PIN lock feature (if enabled).

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
